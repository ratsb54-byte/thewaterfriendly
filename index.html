The source

Natural artesian spring water, bottled small.

Our water is drawn from a protected artesian spring and bottled right at the source in Brampton, Ontario — never touched by anything artificial. We simply portion it into something you'll actually want to carry.

The result tastes the way water should — clean, soft and entirely unremarkable, in the best possible way. Just water, in seventeen languages, and nothing else.
